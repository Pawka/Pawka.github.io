TIL: Copy TMUX pane contents to buffer

2019-09-24

:tmux:

Tmux has a handy shortcut that copies the entire visible contents of a pane to a paste buffer. Enter tmux’s Command mode with <Prefix>:capture-pane. The contents of the pane will be in a paste buffer. You can then paste that content into another pane or window by pressing <Prefix>].
